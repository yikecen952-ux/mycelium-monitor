Label reference
Healthy White Mycelium
White, continuous, relatively intact surface. Matte appearance, no discolouration, no patches of missing coverage.
Dry Aging
Grey-white or cream-yellow tone. Surface has begun to separate, powder, or harden. No longer continuous — partial shrinkage or cracking visible.
Exposed Substrate
Brown wood chips, fibres, or raw interior material visible through the mycelium layer. Mycelium coverage has receded and the substrate is no longer protected.
Contaminated / Infection Risk
Black spots, dark grey, brown-black, or abnormal discolouration. Irregular patches or localised mould growth present. Indicates active biological contamination.
